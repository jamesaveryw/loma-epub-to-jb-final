Beyond Quality

 

In Chapter 1, we stated that quality is one of the primary tenets of successful operations management. To continue delivering value to customers, a company has to improve and maintain the quality of its products and services. Because of strong industry competition, insurance companies are particularly active in supporting quality initiatives and programs.

In this chapter, you will learn how companies define quality and quality management. You’ll also learn how companies implement quality control, use tools for analysis, and address changing customer expectations.

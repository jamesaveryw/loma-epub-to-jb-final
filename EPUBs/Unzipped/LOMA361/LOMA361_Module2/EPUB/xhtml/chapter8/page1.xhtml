Objectives

After studying this chapter, you should be able to

  • 8A Differentiate between the asset valuation reserve (AVR) and the interest maintenance reserve (IMR) and explain the purpose of these two statutory reserves
  • 8B List and describe the three types of policyowner dividend liabilities
  • 8C Explain how an insurer accounts for common types of policyowner dividend payment options
  • 8D Explain the accounting treatment of various nonforfeiture options
  • 8E Describe the death benefit payment process, including the typical accounting entries that an insurer records at each step of the process
  • 8F Explain how an insurer accounts for supplementary contract payments
  • 8G Describe an insurer’s typical liabilities relating to premiums and expenses due or accrued

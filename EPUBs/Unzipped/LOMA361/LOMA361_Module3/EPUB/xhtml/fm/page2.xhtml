Preface

Accounting and Financial Reporting in Life Insurance Companies, 2nd Edition, provides a broad exposure to financial and management accounting in life insurance companies, including the corporate and regulatory environment in which accounting functions occur. The text is comprised of three modules:

  • Module 1: Orientation to Accounting and Financial Reporting (Chapters 1–4)

  • Module 2: Major Accounts and Financial Ratios (Chapters 5–11)

  • Module 3: Management Accounting, Internal Control, and Auditing (Chapters 12–16)

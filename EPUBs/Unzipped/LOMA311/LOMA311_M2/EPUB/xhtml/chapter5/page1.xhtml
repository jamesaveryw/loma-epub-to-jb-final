Objectives

After studying this chapter, you should be able to

  • 5A Describe and recognize an agency relationship between a principal and an agent
  • 5B Identify various types of authority an agent may possess to act on behalf of a principal
  • 5C Describe an agent’s duties to the principal and the principal’s duties to the agent, and identify the remedies available if either party breaches the agency agreement
  • 5D Identify situations in which a principal is liable to a third party with whom an agent deals on the principal’s behalf
  • 5E Identify situations in which an agent acting on behalf of a principal is liable to a third party
  • 5F List the four methods by which an agency relationship can terminate, and explain when a principal has a duty to notify third parties of the termination

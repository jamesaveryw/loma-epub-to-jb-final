Objectives

After studying this chapter, you should be able to

  • 1A Explain why industry professionals need a basic understanding of the legal system
  • 1B Identify the primary sources of law and describe how they are created
  • 1C Explain how the three primary governmental functions are divided among the branches of government
  • 1D Distinguish among the various types of legal systems
  • 1E Describe the organization of the federal and state court systems in the United States and the decision-making procedures that courts follow
  • 1F Define ethics and explain the value of a corporate culture that encourages ethical conduct
  • 1G Describe the purpose of the U.S. Federal Sentencing Guidelines and explain their significance to a company’s compliance and ethics programs

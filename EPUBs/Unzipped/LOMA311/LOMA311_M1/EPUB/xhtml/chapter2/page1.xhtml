Objectives

After studying this chapter, you should be able to

  • 2A Identify the three stages of a civil action and describe the rules and procedures that the civil litigation process follows in the United States
  • 2B Describe the general principles of tort law and explain how liability for tortious conduct can be based on intentional torts, negligence, and strict liability
  • 2C Differentiate between legal remedies and equitable remedies and describe the types of damages that may be awarded in civil actions
  • 2D Describe the equitable principles that equity courts apply to determine whether to grant an equitable remedy to a plaintiff
  • 2E Identify and describe two alternative dispute resolution methods

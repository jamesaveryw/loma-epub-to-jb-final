As we discussed in Chapter 11, when a life insurance policy is issued, most of the rights in the policy belong to the policyowner. However, the policyowner can share the bundle of rights in a policy among many people. In this chapter, we describe some of the ways in which additional parties can gain an interest in a life insurance policy.

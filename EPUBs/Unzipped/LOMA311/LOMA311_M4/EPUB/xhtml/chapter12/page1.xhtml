Objectives

After studying this chapter, you should be able to

  • 12A Identify ways in which the owner of a life insurance policy can transfer ownership of the policy to another person
  • 12B Distinguish between an assignee’s rights under an absolute assignment and a collateral assignment
  • 12C Describe how the rights of a policy beneficiary under an assignment vary according to the type of assignment and the type of beneficiary designation
  • 12D Distinguish between a viatical settlement and a life settlement
  • 12E Explain the rights that creditors of a life insurance policyowner or beneficiary have in the policy
  • 12F Identify the parties to a trust agreement and describe the duties of the trustee and the rights of the trust beneficiary
  • 12G Describe reasons for establishing a trust and distinguish among various types of trust
  • 12H Identify the typical settlement options that a life insurance policy provides and describe the rights of beneficiaries and payees in regard to settlement options

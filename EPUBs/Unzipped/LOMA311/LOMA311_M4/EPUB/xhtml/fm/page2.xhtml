Preface

Business Law for Insurance Professionals provides a basic knowledge of the legal system and the legal and regulatory issues unique to the life insurance industry. The course highlights four major subject areas: the legal environment in which life insurance companies operate; areas of business law; regulation of the life insurance business; and legal issues pertaining to the sale and performance of life insurance and annuity products.

  • Module 1: Life Insurance Companies and Legal Issues (Chapters 1–3)

  • Module 2: Areas of Business Law (Chapters 4–6)

  • Module 3: Regulation of the Life Insurance Business (Chapters 7–9)

  • Module 4: Sale and Performance of Products (Chapters 10-14)

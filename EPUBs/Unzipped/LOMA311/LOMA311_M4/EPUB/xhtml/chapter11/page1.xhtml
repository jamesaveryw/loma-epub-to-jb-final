Objectives

After studying this chapter, you should be able to

  • 11A Describe a policyowner’s rights to name and change the beneficiary of a life insurance policy
  • 11B In a given situation, identify the party to whom life insurance policy proceeds are payable following the insured’s death
  • 11C Distinguish among the types of premiums a policyowner may pay, the payment methods she may use, and the ways an insurer may respond when a premium is unpaid
  • 11D Distinguish among the various nonforfeiture options that insurers provide to owners of cash value life insurance policies
  • 11E Describe the policy loan provision and the policy withdrawal provision in a life insurance policy and distinguish between a policy loan and a commercial loan
  • 11F Describe the accelerated death benefit provision and identify conditions under which the provision may apply
  • 11G Identify and explain the requirements for the reinstatement of a life insurance policy
  • 11H Identify the ownership rights that a contract owner has in an annuity

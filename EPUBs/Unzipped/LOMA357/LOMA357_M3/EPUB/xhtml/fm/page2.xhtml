Preface

Institutional Investing: Principles and Practices, Second Edition introduces students to basic investment principles and the “why” and “how” of institutional investing. Students will learn about institutional investment objectives and explore the institutional investment process. In addition, students will gain an understanding of how a life insurer’s investment function manages investments to support the company’s products and solvency. This text is comprised of four modules:

  • Module 1: The Basics of Investing (Chapters 1–4)

  • Module 2: Planning in Institutional Investing (Chapters 5–6)

  • Module 3: The Institutional Investing Process (Chapters 7–9)

  • Module 4: Regulations and Controls (Chapters 10-11)

After investment professionals construct a portfolio and implement investment strategies, they monitor the portfolio and evaluate its performance. In the first part of this chapter, you will learn how investment professionals monitor portfolios to ensure proper asset allocation and compliance with corporate policies and industry regulations. You will also read about standards that investment professionals use to report portfolio performance.

Next, you will learn about some of the metrics that investment professionals use to measure and evaluate the performance of institutional portfolios. Based on their evaluations, they may implement strategic and tactical adjustments for the portfolios they manage.

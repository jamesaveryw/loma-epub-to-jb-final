Preface

Risk Management and Product Development for Insurance Companies discusses how insurance companies approach the processes of risk management and product development, as well as how the presence of risk informs both general product development and technical product design. The text comprises four modules:

  • Module 1: Risk Management (Chapters 1–4)

  • Module 2: The Product Development Process (Chapters 5–6)

  • Module 3: Factors Considered in Technical Product Design (Chapters 7–9)

  • Module 4: Technical Product Design for Life Insurance and Annuity Products (Chapters 10–11)

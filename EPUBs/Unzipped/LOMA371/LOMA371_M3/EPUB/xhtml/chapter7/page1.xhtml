Objectives

After studying this chapter, you should be able to

  • 7A Describe how an insurer’s general account and separate account affect the insurer’s investment earnings
  • 7B Define investment concepts such as price appreciation, price depreciation, and rate of return
  • 7C Explain the time value of money and the relationship between present value and future value
  • 7D Explain the applications for future values and present values
  • 7E Define a product’s crediting rates and interest spread and explain how an insurer determines these elements for life insurance and annuity products
  • 7F Explain how actuaries account for cash values and accumulated values in product design
  • 7G Describe the effects on insurers when market interest rates are decreasing, increasing, or remaining at a sustained low level
  • 7H Describe common strategies for managing interest-rate risk
  • 7I Define and calculate profitability measures such as net present value, internal rate of return, and profit margin

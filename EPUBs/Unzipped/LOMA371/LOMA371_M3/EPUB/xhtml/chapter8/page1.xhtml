Objectives

After studying this chapter, you should be able to

  • 8A Describe how mortality affects the cost of benefits for life insurance and annuity products and define terms related to mortality
  • 8B Describe a basic process for developing mortality assumptions
  • 8C Identify and perform calculations on data from a mortality table
  • 8D Identify the purposes and characteristics of different types of mortality tables
  • 8E Describe the difference between the mortality patterns of different groups and explain the effect these differences have on product design
  • 8F Describe the projection method and the setback method for modifying tabular mortality rates
  • 8G Describe a process for setting a mortality charge for a life insurance or annuity product and calculate the mortality cost for a life insurance product and an annuity benefit cost for a life annuity
  • 8H Define a mortality margin and describe its use in life insurance and annuity products

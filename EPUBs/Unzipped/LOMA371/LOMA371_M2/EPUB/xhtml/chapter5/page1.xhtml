Objectives

After studying this chapter, you should be able to

  • 5A Discuss the risks associated with launching new products
  • 5B Describe common product development strategies
  • 5C Explain the importance of a product mix and differentiate it from a product portfolio
  • 5D Identify a product’s average shelf life
  • 5E Distinguish between product development projects on the basis of their complexity, and describe the effect complexity has on speed to market
  • 5F Describe how changes in customer experience impact product development
  • 5G Describe the teams that usually participate in the product development process
  • 5H Identify external pressures that influence product development
  • 5I Explain the product planning stage of product development, including idea generation and idea screening
  • 5J Describe the elements of the comprehensive business analysis stage of product development, including a market analysis, product design objectives, a feasibility study, a marketing plan, marketing projections, and a product proposal
